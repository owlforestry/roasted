Roasted (Cocoa)

Roasted (Cocoa) is a simple bootstrapping library for your brand new Mac or spring cleaning. It handles installing all required software, registering their licenses (if possible) and setting your preferences you like. All you need to do is start using your Mac, with your software.

Installation

Add this line to your application's Gemfile:

gem 'roasted'

And then execute:

$ bundle

Or install it yourself as:

$ gem install roasted
